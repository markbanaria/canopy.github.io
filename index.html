ABOUT CANOPY
What We Do
We are a team of technologists, lawyers and all-around intellectual property geeks looking to help companies manage one of their most valuable assets, trade secrets.

We have spent the past year talking to executives at companies large and small, inside and outside legal counsel alike, and trade secret litigators.
The Problem We See
The jury is in…..most companies are doing a poor job of managing their trade secrets.

Many are doing nothing. Many are confused between confidential information and trade secrets. Most don’t even know where to start…..a lack of training, best practices, and systems.
Our Solution
Enter Canopy. Soon you will not only have your legal risk managed, you will see the economic benefit of bringing these otherwise opaque assets to life. More licensing opportunities. New revenue opportunities. Improved enterprise valuations.

Trade secrets are the future of your intellectual property portfolio.
